Who are we ?

Quizzing is an art and a test of the intellect. The Quiz club of the college aims at identifying students with quizzing talent and creating opportunities for them to sharpen their quizzing skills. Quiz

Club is a unique and exciting way to motivate, inspire, encourage and reward children in their quest for knowledge and provide them with the opportunity to celebrate their achievements as part of a high profile, national competition. The main objective of quiz club is to popularize quizzing activities among students and to encourage them to take part in all competitive examination at state and central level.

To instill our students the values of knowledge, wisdom and compassion to make them smart citizens.The purpose of Quizzing club is to provide the students with valuable insight into non-academic aspects of the business so that the students will get an opportunity to mould their career.

Long Live Aec

From the Club's President

Arindam Kashyap Ray

President(2021-22)
AEC QUIZ Club

“ We came, we quizzed, we conquered “

It is indeed a historic moment for AEC Quiz Club, one of the most vibrant quizzing societies of North-East as it finds a proper place in the digital world in the form of this website. Throughout the years, AECQC incessantly strived and laboured to uphold and promote the culture of quizzing and also improving the standard of quizzing in this quest. The memories of AECQC need to be well-documented as it will ensure that the hearts of future AECQC members brim with inspiration and zeal. The stories of our successes - both as organizers and participants will push them to unmatched triumphs and achievements.

The creation of this website is a serious effort on the part of the 2021-22 Core team to chronicle and preserve the legacy of this illustrious club.

From the AECSU's Desk

I believe in multifaceted capabilities of students. It is always a privilege to delve deeper into one's potentials and go ahead with nurturing and culturing of the same during the growing stage of our academic careers. The AEC quiz club is one such platform which provides the students the opportunity to inculcate knowledge, beyond the four walls of a classroom. Being a part of the club as per my portfolio has been an enlightening experience. The quiz activities concerning it's members and their eagerness to participate and bring glory to our college through various events of state and National importance provides a deep sense of satisfaction. I believe the launching of this website shall provide the added boost to bring forth the quizzing talents of the upcoming students, keeping intact the rich legacy of this culture in the history of AEC.
