Emerging Technologies in 2021

A considerable digital revolution is taking place all over the world. Technological advancements have led accelerated market development and innovation. The Covid-19 pandemic has speed up digitization and automation, allowing companies to remain resilient even in adversity. Many businesses adopted new technologies and modified their business models. However, it is not only technology trends and top technologies that are evolving, a lot more has changed in the last two years due to the outbreak of COVID-19 making IT professionals realize that their role will not stay the same in the world tomorrow. It will be a necessity for an IT professional in 2020–21 to be constantly learning, unlearning, and relearning new stuff.
