    Placement Series

    AEC Coding Club along with one of our alumni of the 2018 batch, Mr. Pradipta Sarma (SDE II at FLIPKART) is starting the PLACEMENT PREPARATION SERIES to help the students in upgrading themselves with the current placement scenarios and guide them towards achieving greater heights.

This will be utterly beneficial for students aiming to get placed in their desired companies and will surely help them attain success.
